Easy to Make
How to Cook
1
💧

Soak the Grains

Wash and soak the dal mix in water for 2 hours before cooking.

2
🥩

Add Meat or Veggies

Add mutton, chicken or vegetables along with the masala pack.

3
♨️

Pressure Cook

Pressure cook everything together for 6–7 whistles until soft and rich.

4
🍽️

Garnish & Serve

Top with fried onions, green chillies & ginger julienne. Serve hot!
